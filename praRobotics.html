Who are we?

We're a group of students in Colorado. This website hosts all of our robotics teams for Prospect Ridge Academy. A k-12 school.

What do we do?

We build and code robots of our own to compete in various competitions.

What do we compete in?

  • The Autonomous Vehicle Competition hosted by Sparkfun
  • The First Tech Chanllenge hosted by FIRST
  • The First Lego Leauge also hosted by FIRST
